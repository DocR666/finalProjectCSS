Classic Rock

Classic Rock is what really shaped my music taste. I started by listening to the legends; Led Zeppelin, The Beatles, AC/DC, and others. Classic rock is a genre that defined generations, blending powerful guitar riffs, poetic lyrics, and iconic vocals to create timeless music. Emerging in the 1960s and 70s with bands like Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, and The Beatles, classic rock continues to influence artists across genres.
