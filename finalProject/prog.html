Prog

Prog or progressive music is pushing a genre to its boundaries, and in cases even to new territories. Prog is where music becomes exploration. It's the sound of breaking rules, stretching time, and building entire worlds through sound. Whether it's the soaring epics of 70s prog rock or the crushing, intricate arrangements of modern prog metal, these albums take me somewhere else, and often, challenge me as a guitarist.
